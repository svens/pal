template<typename Protocol>
class pal::net::ip::basic_endpoint< Protocol >

Protocol-specific address/port pair.

Endpoints are used to identify sources and destinations for socket connections and datagrams.

Convert this endpoint to human readable textual representation by filling range [first, last).

On success, returns std::to_chars_result with ptr pointing at past last filled character and ec default value initialized.

On failure, ptr is set to last and ec std::errc::value_too_large. Content of [first, last) is unspecified.
